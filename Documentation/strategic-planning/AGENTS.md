# Strategic Planning

This directory contains exploratory ideas, research findings, and future-looking proposals for Memento. Nothing here is committed to or on any active roadmap — it is a space for capturing thinking before it becomes a spec or ADR.

## What belongs here

- Research results and option analyses for potential improvements
- Architectural ideas that are not yet ready to become ADRs
- Strategy documents exploring future directions (testing, infra, tooling, etc.)

## What does not belong here

- Finalized decisions — those go in `Documentation/ADR/`
- Active implementation plans — those live in the feature directory under `.specify/`
- Step-by-step test instructions — those go in `Documentation/test-instructions/`

## Contents

- `dev-loop-strategy.md` — Research and recommendations for evolving the developer feedback loop (unit tests → automated integration → eval-based tool ergonomics testing)
